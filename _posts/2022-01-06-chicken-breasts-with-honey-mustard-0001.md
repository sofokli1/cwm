### Ingredients
- 3 chicken breast fillets
- 2 tbsp (30ml) olive oil
- 1 tbsp (15ml) salt
- 1 tsp (5ml) pepper
- 4 tbsp (60ml) mustard
- juice from 3 lemons
- 2 tbsp (30ml) honey
